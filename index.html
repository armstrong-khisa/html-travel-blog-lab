Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island National Seashore is a beautiful barrier island located off the southern coast of Long Island, New York. It is known for its sandy beaches, rolling dunes, maritime forests, and the historic Fire Island Lighthouse. Visitors can enjoy hiking, wildlife watching, boating, and relaxing along miles of protected shoreline.

Explore the Adventure!

Whether you're exploring scenic boardwalk trails, visiting the historic lighthouse, or enjoying the peaceful beaches, Fire Island offers a unique outdoor experience. Plan your trip to discover wildlife, nature, and breathtaking ocean views.
